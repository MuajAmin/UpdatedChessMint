What's Inside

Everything You Need to Dominate

A complete chess analysis suite, engineered to give you the ultimate edge on Chess.com.

Real-time Evaluation Bar

Live chess position evaluation with a visual bar showing who's winning. Updates dynamically as pieces move with smooth animations.

+1.45 White advantage

AI Move Hints

Color-coded arrows show you the best moves ranked from best to good to okay. Never miss a great move again.

Legit Auto-Move

Intelligently plays the best move for you with human-like timing variations and randomization to appear natural.

Move Analysis

Every move gets rated — Brilliant, Great, Best, Excellent, Good, Inaccuracy, Mistake, or Blunder — just like Chess.com's review.

Mate Finder

Automatically detects checkmate sequences and alerts you when you're in a forced mate line. Never miss a win.

Text-to-Speech

Hear the best move spoken aloud — perfect for streaming without screen watchers knowing your secret weapon. Uses Google UK English Female voice for natural delivery.

Pre-Moves

Queue up automated pre-moves with configurable delays and randomization for blitz and bullet games.

Config Import/Export

Save and share your perfect settings as a `.mint` config file. Import on any device instantly.

Get Started

Up and Running in 3 Steps

01

Download the Extension

Download the zip from GitHub and unzip it to a folder on your computer. No account or sign-up needed.

Get it on GitHub →
02

Load in Chrome

Go to chrome://extensions, enable Developer Mode, click "Load unpacked", and select the UpdatedChessMint folder.

Chrome URL chrome://extensions/
03

Play on Chess.com

Navigate to Chess.com and start a game. The extension activates automatically. Click the Mint icon to configure settings to your liking.
